About the proejct(s) and people involved

Current mobile friendly application created by a group of developers with deep and extensive experience in Software Development, CAD and GIS. This is a prototype of tools for Geographic Technology industry to provide help with calculations as well as present the data in the convenient way. Now, the calculation of some points in O/H crossing may take 10 min instead of 1 hour. No license for a CAD software required. Able to make it right in the field! If you want to share your thoughts on some improvements of currently presenter tools or there is a need for something outstanding to be developed - you are welcome to share it with us.
